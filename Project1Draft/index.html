Info

Every day, at 00:00 UTC and 12:00 UTC, weather balloons are simultaneously launched from roughly 900 - 1,300 locations around the world. Irregardless of the geopolitical climate, the balloons are launched day in and day out, and the data they gather is shared amongst all of humanity.
